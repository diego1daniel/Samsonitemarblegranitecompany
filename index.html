About Samsonite Marble & Granite

Samosnite Marble & Granite Limited is a leading Nigerian company specializing in the importation, fabrication, supply, and installation of premium marble, granite, and natural stone surfaces. Established in 2009, we have built a strong reputation for quality, reliability, and craftsmanship in marble and granite projects across residential, commercial, and hospitality sectors.
