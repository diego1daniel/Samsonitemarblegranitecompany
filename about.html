Market Analysis

Industry Overview

Nigeria's marble and granite industry has witnessed significant growth due to the expansion of the construction, real estate, and hospitality sectors. The rising demand for luxury finishes, increased urbanization, and the emergence of new housing estates have fueled consumption of stone materials.

The Nigerian Bureau of Statistics (NBS) projects construction sector growth at over 10% annually, with luxury building materials like marble and granite accounting for approximately N50 billion (about 35,000,000 USD) of the market share. Samsonite is well-positioned to tap into this growth, leveraging its decade-long operational success and brand equity.

Market Trends

There is a growing demand for imported natural stones due to superior quality, this has increased the preference for marble and granite over tiles and ceramics. Couple with the rising investments in commercial buildings, hotels, and estates, this has led to a paradigm shift toward aesthetic interior and exterior finishes.
